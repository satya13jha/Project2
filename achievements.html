Paradise Hotels is India's third largest hotel chain with over 100 hotels.It is based in the Hotels Division Headquarters at Kolkata, West Bengal. It is part of the JK Limited group of companies. Paradise Hotels is regularly voted amongst the best employers in Asia in the hospitality sector.JK Limited entered the hotel business on 18 October 2000 with the opening of a hotel in Chennai.Paradise Hotels has a reputation of playing host to visiting royalty and world leaders time and again.
